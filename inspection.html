The first shipment consisted of 65,000 tons of coal from the BHP Mines in South and East Kalimantan. In 2002, the company accounted for 45% of all Indonesian coal export superintending.

Inspection certificates from PT GEOSERVICES are widely recognized as having the assurance of integrity. They are accepted and valued by trading houses, sellers, buyers, producers, banks, and governments.

Based on the long-term success in coal superintending, PT GEOSERVICES have expanded their high quality services into other fields over the past decades.

PT GEOSERVICES is licensed by PERTAMINA to provide quality and quantity surveys for Crude Oil, Condensate, LPG, Petroleum Products, and Petrochemicals.

Scope of services for Collateral Management:

PT Geoservices will act as Collateral Manager on the request of a financial institution, when physical commodities under collateral are received into a storage facility and retained under full custody until the Collateral Manager is instructed by the financial institution to release (part of) such commodities to the depositor and/or ultimate receiver of the goods.

- Storage facility inspection assuring suitability for storing commodities

- Supervision, recording and reporting of 100% weighing of incoming commodities into storage facilities

- Issuance of Warehouse/ Silo Receipts to financial institution

- Around the clock custody of the commodities stored (under lock and key)

- Daily condition and stock movement checks and reporting to financial institution/ depositor

- Supervision, recording and reporting of 100% weighing of outgoing commodities upon receipt of written Release Orders from the financial institution

Products handled: grains, feedstuffs, oilseeds, sugar, fertilizer, tobacco, spices, nuts and cocoa

Scope of services for Stock Monitoring:

To provide stock monitoring services for verification of quality and quantity of commodities stored in warehouses and/or silos. The Stock Monitoring Agreement is signed between the parties involved in the stock monitoring. The commodities under SMA are not held under exclusive custody. The documents related to commodities storage/movement are verified and validated. The incoming and outgoing movement of commodities supervised. All recordings are kept and reported on a periodic basis.

Products handled: grains, feedstuffs, oilseeds, sugar, fertilizer, tobacco, spices, nuts and cocoa
