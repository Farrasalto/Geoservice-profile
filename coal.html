Coal

In 1982, PT GEOSERVICES was established its coal laboratory under the terms of a Technical Assistance Agreement with the Australian Coal Industrial Research Laboratory (ACIRL). Highly trained and experienced Indonesian staff perform all work in duplicate, in accordance with internationally accepted standards: ASTM, British Standard (BS), and International Standards (ISO), that was the Geoservices coal laboratory begin.

Now, Quality Control is maintained by daily analysis of standard samples, the results of which are presented as “range charts”, and by participation in regular “round robin” testing program. Balikpapan, Banjarbaru, Samarinda, and NPLCT are accredited to ISO 17025.

Services

SAMPLING

- Run Of Mine (ROM)

- Production coal from conveyor belts or falling streams

- Stockpiles

- Coal preparation plant feed, product, and refuse streams

- Slurry

- In-pit channels sampling

- Pre-shipment inspection

COAL PREPARATION STUDIES

- Particle size distribution

- Washability for plant performance or plant design

- Sample pretreatment (adjustment of particle size distribution and treatment in water) as prescribed in relevant Australian Standard.

COAL ANALYSIS

- Complete evaluation of thermal coals, including:

- Total moisture

- Proximate analysis

- Colorific value

- Hardgrove grindability index

- Ultimate analysis

- Ash analysis

- Ash fusion temperatures

- Trace Element Analysis

Balikpapan laboratory also has the capacity to evaluate metallurgical coals, including:

- Crucible swelling number

- Gieseler fluidity

- Gray King Coke Type

CONSULTANCY

The Technical Advisor for Coal Testing can assist with the design of coal testing programs for either exploration or production samples, and provide expert analysis in the interpretation of the results.

REPORTING

Reporting requirements will be discussed individually with each client. Progress reports can be provided by email or fax. Final reports are provided in hard copy in accordance with the client’s requested format. CD or floppy copies are also available.
